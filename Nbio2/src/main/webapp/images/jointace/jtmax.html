Jointace Max

Maintaining an active lifestyle

Cartilage and bone health are important for long term wellbeing. Jointace MAX has been developed by Vitabiotics nutritional experts and provides unique combinations of trace minerals, vitamins and nutrients, ideal for those with an active life, in sport or in later life.

Jointace MAX

Jointace MAX is the ultimate product in the Jointace range, and is designed for those who want the most comprehensive support possible. It combines Collagen, Glucosamine, Turmeric and Chondroitin tablets with super strength Omega-3 capsules in a special triple pack. Jointace MAX contains a truly comprehensive combination and its 3 in 1 approach provides:

Glucosamine & Chondroitin

Along with the trace minerals, Jointace MAX contains the Jointace Original tablet providing Glucosamine present in the preferred potassium form, and a high quality LMW (low molecular weight) form of Chondroitin, together with Ginger Root extract and Turmeric extract, well-known botanical ingredients. In addition, Jointace MAX also provides super strength Omega-3 fish oil capsules plus Jointace Collagen tablets.

Super strength Omega-3 fish oil

The Jointace Max Omega-3 capsule provides a rich source of DHA (Docosahexaenoic Acid), and is produced to the highest quality and purity standards. The fish oil we use is certified for sustainability by Friend of the Sea.

Collagen (Type II)

Jointace Collagen tablets provide a high-grade collagen which is hydrolysed to improve absorption.

Trace minerals and vitamins

Copper contributes to the maintenance of normal connective tissues and therefore can be beneficial to cartilage (as cartilage is made up of connective tissues).
Vitamin D, zinc and manganese all help to maintain normal bones. Vitamin D3 is provided in its Cholecalciferol form as naturally produced by the body on exposure to sunlight.
Vitamin C contributes to normal collagen formation for the normal function of bones and cartilage.

Jointace Max FAQ

Why has Jointace MAX been developed?

Jointace MAX combines collagen tablets, Glucosamine, Turmeric & Chondroitin tablets and Omega-3 capsules in an ultimate triple pack formula scientifically developed to help safeguard your diet with specific nutrients to help support normal cartilage and bone health which are important for long term wellbeing and maintaining an active lifestyle.

Can Jointace MAX be used simultaneously with medicines?

Jointace MAX is a food supplement and contains only moderate levels of vitamins and minerals. For specific advice on taking Jointace MAX with medicines, we recommend that you contact your doctor or pharmacist first. Diabetics and those using blood thinning medication should consult their doctor before using this product.

How many tablets and capsules does one pack of Jointace MAX contain?

Jointace MAX contains 28 omega-3 capsules, 28 Glucosamine, Turmeric, Chondroitin tablets and 28 collagen tablets, which are taken one per day.

How and when should Jointace MAX be used?

Take one Omega-3 capsule (Turquoise), one Glucosamine tablet (dark blue blister) and one collagen tablet (pink blister). These are recommended to be taken with or immediately after your main meal, with water or a cold drink. Jointace MAX tablets/capsules should not be chewed. Taking Jointace MAX with a large meal maximises the absorption of the nutrients and can reduce nausea, which is sometimes experienced if multivitamins and fish oil are taken on an empty stomach. Always ensure that Jointace MAX tablets and capsules are taken with plenty of liquid to wash the tablets down, and not tea or coffee, as this may cause the tablets and capsules to disintegrate too early.

Are there any undesired effects whilst taking Jointace MAX?

Jointace MAX tablets and capsules have no known side-effects when taken as directed. Do not exceed the recommended intake. In case of overdose, seek medical advice immediately. Jointace MAX is not suitable for use by those people allergic to soya, shellfish or fish products. Diabetics should consult their doctor before using this product. Jointace MAX is not recommended during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Consult your doctor before use if taking oral anticoagulants.

Need help swallowing a tablet or capsule?

Difficulty in swallowing tablets or capsules is quite a common experience. Vitabiotics have researched this extensively and recommend after chewing some food (e.g. banana), just before you swallow, pop the tablet or capsule into your mouth and swallow it together with the chewed food, followed by some liquid. The throat opens up wider to swallow the chewed food and so the tablet or capsule should go down with greater ease. Alternatively, the tablet or capsule may be swallowed with thicker liquids such as yoghurts or smoothies.

What Are The Nutrients Included?

The key nutrients found in our Jointace Max product includes Glucosamine, Turmeric, Chondroitin, Omega 3 Fish oil, Collagen Type II complex, vitamin D, vitamin E, vitamin C, folic acid, vitamin B12, zinc, copper, selenium and Ginger Root extract. To see the full list of ingredients for this product please click on the nutritional information tab found on this listing.

For how long should Jointace MAX be used?

Results vary amongst individuals, but in most cases, we would expect the beneficial effects of the nutrients and bioactive elements in Jointace MAX to build over one to three months. There is no maximum length of time over which Jointace MAX may be used. A regular intake is recommended.

Some Nutrient Reference Value (NRV) levels are above 100%, is this safe?

Certain vitamins are very safe at levels tens or even hundreds of times the NRV, whereas for others the safety level is much closer to the NRV. For this reason every vitamin and mineral needs to considered on an individual basis in terms of its upper safe level.

The Nutrient Reference Value (NRV) is the amount of a vitamin or mineral which has been calculated to help prevent basic deficiency states in the general population. However, certain vitamins and minerals at optimal levels, above the NRV, have consistently been shown to have positive benefits in specific areas of health. This is why some Vitabiotics formulas have certain nutrient levels greater than 100% NRV.

All Vitabiotics products have been developed using the latest research and contain effective levels of vitamins and minerals, while avoiding any excessive amounts, in accordance with official upper safe limits for vitamins and minerals.

Vitabiotics avoids ‘mega dose' levels of isolated nutrients and instead we have always stressed the importance of safe, moderate and sensible levels of vitamins and minerals, carefully balanced to maximise their combined effect; an approach which has been supported by many experts and researchers over the years.

How to subscribe to Jointace Max?

Each Jointace Max pack contains 28 days supply (4 weeks). We recommend subscribing to 1 pack every 4 weeks.
